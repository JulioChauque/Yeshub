About

Company Overview

Who we are
and what we do

YES (Young Entrepreneurial Solutions E.I.) is a multidisciplinary company focused on delivering practical business solutions through strategic communication, digital innovation, and entrepreneurial thinking.


Originally established with a focus on communication, marketing, tourism, and event support, YES has expanded into technology — website development, app solutions, software support, and the practical integration of AI tools to enhance productivity and innovation.


The company operates with a flexible, solution-oriented approach, adapting services to each client's objectives and environment.
